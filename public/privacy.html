Privacy policy

Last updated 12 March 2026

This policy explains what information Loomwell collects when you use our store, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We keep it short and try to avoid jargon.

Information we collect

  • Account details you provide, such as your name and email address.
  • Order and shipping information needed to fulfil a purchase.
  • Basic technical data, such as your browser type and pages visited, used to keep the site working.

How we use it

We use your information to process orders, provide customer support, send order updates, and improve the store. If you opt in, we may send occasional emails about new arrivals. You can unsubscribe at any time.

How we protect it

Passwords are stored using one-way hashing and are never kept in plain text. Access to personal data is limited to what is needed to run the store. We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete your account. To make a request, email privacy@loomwell.co.

Cookies

We use a small amount of local browser storage to keep you signed in and to remember your cart and theme preference. No third-party advertising cookies are used.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us through the contact page or at privacy@loomwell.co.
