Shipping & returns

Updated for the current season

Shipping

Orders are packed and dispatched within one to two business days. Standard delivery takes three to five business days. Shipping is free on orders over $150; otherwise a flat rate of $8.95 applies at checkout.

  • Standard: 3 to 5 business days
  • Express: 1 to 2 business days, calculated at checkout
  • Free standard shipping over $150

Tracking your order

Once your order ships, we email a confirmation. If you have an account, you can also see the status under my orders.

Returns

If something is not right, you have 30 days from delivery to return it. Items should be unworn, with tags attached, and in their original packaging. Once we receive the return, refunds are processed to the original payment method within five business days.

How to start a return

Email returns@loomwell.co with your order number and the items you would like to return, and we will send a prepaid label.

Exchanges

The quickest way to exchange a size is to return the original item and place a new order, so the piece you want is not held up while the return is in transit.
